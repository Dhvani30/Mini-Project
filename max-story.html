Max's Journey to Mobility

Meet Max, a spirited terrier whose life was transformed by Pawthos. After a tragic accident, Max faced the daunting prospect of limited mobility. Pawthos stepped in, providing Max with a custom-designed prosthetic limb, enabling him to rediscover the joy of running and playing. This is Max's story of resilience, hope, and the unwavering support that changed his life.
