Frequently Asked Questions

How can I support Pawthos?

You can support Pawthos by donating, volunteering your time, or spreading the word about our mission. Every contribution helps us provide prosthetic limbs and care to animals in need.

What types of animals do you help?

We help a wide variety of animals, including dogs, cats, birds, and even farm animals. Our goal is to improve the mobility and quality of life for any animal that can benefit from a prosthetic device.

How long does it take to get a prosthetic?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the case. After an initial consultation and casting, the custom fabrication process typically takes between 2 to 4 weeks.
