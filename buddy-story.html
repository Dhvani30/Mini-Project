Buddy's Journey

Buddy, a spirited Labrador, faced a life-altering challenge when he lost his leg in an accident. His zest for life, however, remained undiminished. With the support of Pawthos, Buddy received a custom-fitted prosthetic limb, enabling him to run, play, and enjoy life to the fullest once again. This is his story of resilience, hope, and the transformative power of compassion.
