Transforming Lives, One Step at a Time

At Pawthos, we believe every animal deserves a chance to live a full and active life. Our mission is to provide prosthetic limbs and ongoing support to animals in need, helping them regain their mobility and rediscover their joy.

Success Stories

Buddy's Journey to Mobility

Buddy, a spirited Labrador, lost his leg in an accident. With a custom prosthetic from Pawthos, he's back to chasing squirrels and enjoying life to the fullest.

Whiskers' Second Chance

Whiskers, a resilient feline, faced amputation due to a severe injury. Our team fitted her with a prosthetic, and she's now exploring her world with renewed vigor.

Spirit's Triumph Over Adversity

Spirit, a majestic mare, suffered a debilitating leg injury. Thanks to Pawthos's expertise, she's galloping again, embodying resilience and hope.
